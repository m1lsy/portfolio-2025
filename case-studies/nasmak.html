Nasmak PM Media Campaign

Client

Nasmak PM, Eindhoven

Timeline

Nov - Dec 2024

Skills

JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Git

Overview

Building on our successful media campaign for Nasmak PM, I developed an interactive web application called PMRecorder that allows the band to create custom, animated videos for their social media platforms. This tool enables them to create engaging content featuring their brand mascots, helping them reach and engage with younger audiences.

Overview

I built a media campaign for Nasmak PM, an experimental electronic band, to help them attract younger audiences, grow their fanbase, and establish a stronger social media presence. The project focused on creating a distinctive visual identity that matched their unique sound while appealing to a new generation of music fans.

Research Process

Understanding the Audience

The research phase focused on understanding both current and potential audiences:

Current Audience Profile

  • Age: 40-60 years old
  • Gender: Male-dominated fanbase
  • Location: Predominantly Netherlands-based
  • Music Preferences: Punk, post-punk, garage rock

Youth Engagement Analysis

Research revealed three key platforms for reaching younger audiences:

  • TikTok: Primary platform for Gen Z, ideal for viral music content
  • Instagram: Essential for visual storytelling and real-time engagement
  • YouTube: Crucial for long-form content and music discovery

First-Hand Experience

To deeply understand the band's essence, I attended their rehearsals with teammates. This immersive experience provided valuable insights into their unique energy and style, while also creating opportunities to capture authentic content for promotional materials.

Development Process

1. Project Planning

Following our media campaign work, my teammate and I identified an opportunity to help Nasmak PM create more engaging social media content. Despite our limited JavaScript experience, we decided to challenge ourselves by creating an interactive app that would bring their brand mascots to life.

2. Prototyping Journey

The development process involved three key prototypes:

  • Initial proof of concept with basic shapes and movement
  • Second iteration adding sprite functionality
  • Final prototype with full character control and size adjustments

3. Final Implementation

The final version of PMRecorder includes:

  • Intuitive control panel for sprite manipulation
  • Character toggle functionality
  • Size and speed adjustments
  • Background color changer with green screen support
  • One-click recording and download capabilities

4. Version Control

We implemented Git workflows to manage our collaborative development:

  • Shared GitLab repository for code synchronization
  • Descriptive commit messages for clear change tracking
  • Conflict resolution using Git's version history
  • Learning experience in collaborative coding practices
"I am very pleased with this application as it is something that we can use right away. I am very excited to show the rest of the band members!" — Toon, Nasmak PM

Results & Recognition

Conducted user testing with diverse demographics to validate designs:

Key Findings

  • Strong positive response to the fisheye effect and bold graphics
  • Suggestions for more vibrant colors to enhance impact
  • Recommendations for interactive elements like QR codes
"Both posters effectively represent the band's aesthetic. The fisheye effect in Poster 2 is eye-catching, and I like that Poster 1 has similarities to our music video. Overall, the designs are very impressive!" — Nasmak PM

Growth Strategy

Implemented practical growth tactics including:

  • Partnership with @eindhovencity for free event promotion
  • Strategic poster placement in Strijp S area
  • Cross-platform content distribution plan
  • Development of consistent brand mascots

Reflection

This project was particularly challenging and rewarding due to the unique nature of the client. The band's 'weird yet cool' vibe required a careful balance between creativity and professionalism. The success of the campaign, as evidenced by the client's positive feedback, demonstrated our ability to capture their distinctive style while creating content that would resonate with a younger audience.
